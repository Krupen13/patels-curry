Pickup & Delivery Service

At Patel's Curry, we're committed to bringing the flavors of India right to your doorstep with our convenient pickup and delivery options.

Pickup:

Experience the convenience of our pickup service by placing your order online or over the phone. Simply select your desired items from our menu, choose a pickup time that works for you, and our team will have your order ready for pickup at our restaurant. Skip the line and enjoy your favorite dishes from the comfort of your home or office.

Delivery:

Can't make it to our restaurant? No problem! We offer reliable delivery service to bring our delicious Indian cuisine directly to you. Whether you're craving a hearty curry, sizzling tandoori, or indulgent dessert, our delivery team will ensure that your order arrives fresh and on time. Simply place your order online or through our delivery partners, sit back, and let us take care of the rest.

Safety Measures:

Rest assured, we prioritize the health and safety of our customers and staff. Our pickup and delivery processes are designed with strict hygiene protocols in place, including contactless delivery options and regular sanitization of our premises and delivery vehicles. Your satisfaction and well-being are our top priorities.
